How it works ↓

How it works

Two phases. One goal.

You’re the prosecutor. The LLM plays everyone else: characters in investigation, the opposing lawyer in court, and the judge—all constrained by the case file.

Investigation

Interrogate characters and unlock evidence. The LLM roleplays each witness within the case’s defined truth.

Courtroom

Present your case. The LLM plays the defense attorney (rebuttals) and the judge, who rules on the facts.

Fully offline

Runs with local Ollama—no paid APIs. Python 3.10+ and standard library.

Deterministic verdict

Rule-based scoring: critical evidence, weights, and chains. The LLM explains; it doesn’t invent.

In the box

Everything you need.

  • Multiple cases: The Stolen Pen, The Missing Lunch, The Broken Vase, The Copied Report, The Stolen Keys
  • Two phases: Investigation (question budget, evidence unlock) and Courtroom (present, rebut, close)
  • Configurable Ollama models for NPCs, defense, and judge
  • Dry-run mode for testing without an LLM
